## About Volume 140

Volume 140 of the Patrologia Latina covers authors from the 11th century, particularly from the German lands and Low Countries. This includes works by Adelbold II, Bishop of Utrecht, who corresponded with Pope Sylvester II (Gerbert of Aurillac) on mathematical topics.
