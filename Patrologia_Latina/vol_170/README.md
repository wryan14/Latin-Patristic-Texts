### Rupert of Deutz - Dispute Between a Monk and a Cleric: Whether a Monk May Preach

**Latin Title:** Altercatio Monachi et Clerici
**Author:** Rupert of Deutz (c. 1075â€“1129)
**Date:** c. 1120 CE
**Genre:** Theological dialogue

A 12th-century dialogue defending the right of ordained monks to preach against Jerome's dictum that monks should "weep, not teach."
